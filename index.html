About me

Javiera Leemhuis

I hold a BSc in Mathematics and Physics from the University of Warwick. In 2020, I was awarded funding by the ESRC to complete 4-year PhD with an integrated Masters in Data Analytics and Society. This exciting opportunity allows me to combine my love for the natural and social sciences. In particular, I'm is interested in using data to drive policy and decision making that will have a real impact on people’s health and wellbeing. My PhD research is focused on how a person’s experience of providing care, or living with a disability/ needing care and support is shaped by where they live. I will be working in collaboration with the Office for National Statistics (ONS), who is also sponsoring the project.
